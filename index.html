Connect Google Drive
This takes about 5 minutes and is free. Your dashboard data will save to a file in your Google Drive automatically.
1
Go to console.cloud.google.com and sign in with your Google account.
2
Create a new project (name it anything, e.g. "My Dashboard").
3
Go to APIs & Services → Library, search for "Google Drive API" and enable it.
4
Go to APIs & Services → Credentials → Create Credentials → OAuth client ID. Choose "Web application". Under Authorized JavaScript origins add: http://localhost and the URL where you host this file.
5
Copy the Client ID and paste it below.
